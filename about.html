About Kingdom Care Foundation

The Kingdom Care Foundation aims at contributing to an improved future for Nigerians and other African citizens. To achieve this mission, the foundation creates and support initiatives that bring about development, productivity, opportunities and job creation in the following areas:

  • AGRICULTURAL EMPOWERMENT PROGRAMMES
  • SKILLS ACQUISITION PROGRAMMES
  • CLEAN WATER PROJECTS
  • SCHOOLS PROJECTS
  • HEALTHCARE SERVICES

The organization forms strategic partnerships involving stakeholders and beneficiaries. The foundation director leverages knowledge of relevant stakeholder’s working protocols, structures and decision-making processes to identify candidates likely to partner with the foundation to create and launch programs. Critical relationships and partnerships with grantees are managed to achieve targets by doing site visits and through continuous evaluation and monitoring of grant components.

About The Founder / Biography

Dr Chidozie Wilson Nwankwo is a Nigerian Business man, Philanthropist, and the chairman of WICHTECH GROUP OF COMPANIES.

Dr Nwankwo is the founder of Wichtech Industries Ltd; the best-known company in the group with over 160 offices in Nigeria, Ghana and Ivory Coast has built a reputation for distributing specialized quality Building products such as stone coated roofing sheets (Gerard products) from Fletcher Building Ltd. • IPS plumbing solutions • Betek paints • Wichtech Homes; developing Estates in Ikoyi, Lagos and Abuja • Oakland Engineering Limited; construction services •AIN WILSON SERVICES LTD; Oil and Gas services.

Born 12 September 1965.

He is a graduate of Building Services Engineering from Greenhills, University of Denmark. He was awarded a PhD from Green Hills (University of Denmark) in April 2013 for Leadership, Community Development and International Relations, and another Honorary Doctorate degree in December 2015 from Commonwealth University Belize. Studied Building Technology under City and Guilds, London. He has also attended many professional courses both in the Europe and United States of America.

Due to his philanthropic nature, Dr Nwankwo established the Kingdom Care Foundation, a non-profit making, non-governmental organization (NGO) that organises poverty alleviation programs, offer scholarships and supporting orphans and widows in the rural landscape of Nigeria. The foundation has recorded tremendous breakthroughs in positively affecting millions of lives since inception 30 years ago.

Dr Nwankwo is married and blessed with Children.
