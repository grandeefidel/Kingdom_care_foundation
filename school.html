MODERN SCHOOLS PROJECTS

Education is the foundation of all development and a vital catalyst for growth. It creates choices and opportunities for people, reduces the twin burdens of poverty and diseases, and gives a stronger voice in society. Education is proven to be the key to ensuring sustained and equitable economic growth, improved health and social development.

Study shows that providing every mother with secondary education would save the lives of 1.8 million children every year.

This day and every day, about 8.2 million children in Nigeria alone will wake up with no prospect of a stimulating and life-enhancing school day ahead, facing hunger, hard work, ill-health and poverty as a result. Each of these children may never know the inside of a classroom, may never read a textbook and unfortunately may never know the care of a good teacher. They wonâ€™t be able to experience the benefits of being able to count, read, develop life skills, and will miss out on the job opportunities and improved health afforded by a good quality education.

In Nigeria today, a lot of existing government schools lack conducive environment and the right tools for learning. In order to reduce this problem, Kingdom Care Foundation is committed to providing tools and materials for learning like Textbooks, Notebooks, other Writing materials, School bags, School uniforms, computer and other school facilities. We also go as far as equipping the classrooms with materials needed for convenient and effective learning. In addition, we go ahead to reconstruct or renovate an entire school if found in a deplorable state or position. Our joy is always complete when we look at the beautiful faces of those kids excited to be in School and having a good time looking all nice in their school uniforms. Every Child deserves the opportunity of a conducive environment for learning. They still remain the leaders of tomorrow.
