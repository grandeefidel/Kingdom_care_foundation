CHARITY BEGINS AT HOME

The easiest way to Fight Poverty and Unemployment is "EMPOWERMENT" -Dr.Chidozie Nwankwo.

They say charity begins at home; if you're going to change the world, you have to begin from home.

As I send my thoughts and prayers to you using this medium to communicate, I also see your concerns and pleas in the comments session. This concern is the reason I started thinking of a better way to send relief to the general public to let them know that in all these trials, there is still hope.

15,000 pieces of hand sanitizers was dispersed as a measure to help as many individuals as possible to maintain good hygiene, and I also sent out over 1,500 5kg bags of Rice as a way to help provide succor to the people of Anambra who may be vulnerable in times like this.

On this note, I implore every well-meaning individual to continue to pray for our state Anambra and our country Nigeria, and I also ask that you maintain and observe every safety measure put in place by the government.

I know we've been through a lot, but together we will overcome these trying times in Jesus' name. Amen.

My thoughts and prayers are always with you. Be strong. Be safe.
